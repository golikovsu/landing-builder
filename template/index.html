Frequently asked

Is IQ Option safe? Is my money protected? +

Yes. IQ Option is regulated by CySEC (license #247/14) and operates under EU financial laws. Client funds are held in segregated accounts, completely separate from company funds. Your balance is protected up to €20,000 under the Investor Compensation Fund.

How much do I need to start trading? +

The minimum deposit is $10. Individual trades can be as small as $1. You can also practice as long as you need on the demo account with $10,000 virtual funds — completely free, no deposit required.

How fast are withdrawals processed? +

Withdrawal requests are processed within 1 business day. The funds then take 1–5 business days to arrive depending on your payment method. Bank cards: 1–3 days. E-wallets (Skrill, Neteller): same day. Crypto: within hours.

What assets can I trade on IQ Option? +

Over 500 instruments including: Forex pairs (70+), Stocks (100+), Crypto CFDs (30+), Commodities (gold, oil, silver), Stock indices, ETFs, and Options (where available). New assets are added regularly.

Do I need trading experience to start? +

No experience needed. The free demo account lets you learn with zero risk. We also offer a built-in education center, video tutorials, market analysis tools, and AI-powered signals to guide beginners. Most users place their first trade within 10 minutes.

Is the platform available on mobile? +

Yes. IQ Option has native apps for iOS and Android, both rated 4.4+ stars. All features available on desktop are also available on mobile — including charts, signals, withdrawals, and tournaments. The app is free to download.
